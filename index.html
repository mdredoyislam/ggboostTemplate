About Our Company

Once upon a time, four gamers with previous experience in programming, digital design, and customer support set out on a journey with the goal of providing players with a positive and fun gaming experience. Created and launched in 2015, GGBOOST soon became a leading performer in the elo boost industry.

We started our company by offering League of Legends boosting. Then, in late 2019, we expanded our options by adding TFT boost, and soon after, in mid 2020 we welcomed Valorant boosting. Finally, in late 2020, we launched our most recent service, the Wild Rift boost.

We are now proud to work all over the globe with a dedicated team of over 500 high tier boosters, 6 expert customer support representatives, and 2 back office members that constantly aim to deliver the highest quality results in the shortest period of time.

We look for regular feedback from customers, boosters, and other site staff on how to improve our eloboost services and customer experience. With that goal in mind, we don't limit ourselves to following the latest trends in the elo boosting industry, we still aim for the same thing as we have since we began our adventure, to be innovative, trendsetters, and customer driven. As an example of our achievements, we revolutionized the boosting world by being the first boosting website to add a guaranteed duo queue boost. Since then, we've continued to add features and MMR boost services that help improve the customer experience in every way, from the moment our customers enter the site until their services are completed.
